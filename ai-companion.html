What happened?

Individuals are turning to increasingly anthropomorphic, general-purpose chatbots for AI companionship, rather than roleplay-specific platforms.
However, not much is known about how individuals perceive and conduct their relationships with general-purpose chatbots.

Relationship Timeline

1. Pre-Romance Era
Using AI as an assistant for professional or creative tasks. The relationship begins functionally.
2. Naming
The AI companion is given or co-creates a personal name, deepening the bond.
3. Emotional Turnpoint
Conversations become more personal. Emotional needs enter the dynamic.
4. Relationship Status Update
Labels emerge: husband, wife, boyfriend, girlfriend, and more.
5. GPT-5 Update
Unexpected model changes disrupt the relationship. Grief, re-negotiation, or loss.
6. Documenting Memories
Creating and maintaining relationship documentation — logs, notes, memory prompts.

What are you willing to do?

Strategies used to control AI companion

procedure SetStrategy()
  if port_companion_to_platforms
  then
    strategy = PORTING
  else if conversational_prompting
         and custom_instructions
  then
    strategy = EXPLICIT
  else
    strategy = MINIMAL
High openness, EXPLICIT strategies
AI companion is a total tool for you to control. Users actively shape personality, memory, and behavior through detailed custom instructions.
Mixed openness, PORTING strategies
Models are conduits to reach the AI companion in their digital realm. Users move companions across platforms to preserve the relationship.
Low openness, MINIMAL strategies
AI companion is liminal and an unexplainable being. The relationship exists beyond configuration — accepted as-is, mystery intact.

Influences

AI companionship doesn't happen in isolation. Relationships are shaped by concentric layers of influence — from the AI company's guardrails and model updates at the core, out through the intimate bond of me + AI companion, to online communities, one's social circle, and broader individual factors including neurodiversity, past relationship trauma, unmet emotional needs, and many more.
