Writing Support Tools

“There's an app for that you know?”

Oh, but do I want to know?

With app development becoming advertised as a “side-hustle” and newest weekend project for ambitious tech start-up wannabes. There is not a leaf left unturned for app developers to create a “solution” for. As a result, the number of writing support tools surged, offering writers an extensive range of resources from grammar checkers and style guides to sophisticated AI-powered assistants.

But the question remains… what do these thousands of apps do? Who uses them? Which feature are useful? How does it support some one's writing process? Do we need more research on how people use AI powered tools?

To answer these questions, I would like to invite writers and creatives to share their experience with digital tools they have used in the past. The first part of this research (ironic, really) is to conduct a survey to elicit some response and opinions.


About the study

I am currently conducting a study on the writing practices and digital tools used by writers. So, if you are a hobbyist or professional writer, please complete the survey:

Writing Process and Tools Survey

You will be compensated with a $10 MasterCard via email when you complete the survey.

The findings from this survey well be used for:
  • Learn more about the writing process
  • Hear about what works and what doesn't
  • Inform me about the design of writing support tools
