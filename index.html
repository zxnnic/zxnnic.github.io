Welcome to My Website

My name is Zixin (read as zee-shin) and I occasionally am refered as Nicole. I'm currently a graduate student completing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Toronto under the joint supervision of Fanny Chevalier and Gerald Penn. I'm working on systems to support creative writing and am interested in looking at language variation over time. I previously completed a MSc at Ontario Tech University under the supervision of Christopher Collins developing a gaze based reading aid for language learners. I completed a BSc majoring is in Electrical Engineering and minoring in Computing Science. I am currently working developing writing support tools for fiction authors and exploring languages spoken by immigrants in North America! Send me a message if this is something you're interested in, I'd love to chat :)

My main research areas are HCI, visualization, NLP, and language learning. You can read through my CV to get a better idea of what I've done.

Feel free to reach out! Until next time :-)
